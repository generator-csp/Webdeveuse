📞 Immediate Help – India

If you're located in India, here are some trusted mental health helplines available 24/7:

📞 iCall – Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS)

Number: +91 9152987821
Email: icall@tiss.edu
Service: Free, confidential, professional support in multiple languages.

📞 Vandrevala Foundation Mental Health Helpline

Number: 1860 266 2345 or 1800 233 3330
Website: www.vandrevalafoundation.com
Service: 24x7 support for mental health emergencies.

📞 AASRA

Number: +91-9820466726
Email: aasrahelpline@yahoo.com
Service: Suicide prevention and emotional support.

🌐 Global Helplines

If you're outside India, please refer to these global helpline directories:

  • 🌍 Befrienders Worldwide
  • 🌍 Lifeline International
  • 🌍 International Mental Health Helplines

🚑 When to Seek Emergency Help?

  • Thoughts of self-harm or suicide
  • Severe panic attacks or emotional breakdown
  • Being a danger to yourself or others
  • Witnessing someone in a mental health crisis

🛑 Important Note:

This platform is not a substitute for emergency or crisis care. In life-threatening situations, please contact your local emergency number immediately.
